Legionen - The Game

Project members: 4

This game was project made by part of the welcoming committee for the new students at MSc Media Technology. It’s a 2D pixel art game where the player plays as one of the people in the welcoming committee. The aim is to collect as many pineapples and new students as possible within the time limit and avoid the dangers.

Together with one other person we developed the back-end of the game. Unity was used to develop the game.
